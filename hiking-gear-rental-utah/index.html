Good to know

Hiking rental FAQs

Where can I rent hiking gear in Utah?+

Take a Hike Rentals rents hiking and day-hike gear with local pickup in Saratoga Springs, Utah — minutes from Lehi, American Fork, Provo and the Wasatch trailheads. Reserve online and pick up on your hike dates.

What hiking gear can I rent?+

Daypacks and multi-day backpacks, trekking poles, water filters, satellite communicators, first-aid kits, bear spray and traction gear for winter trails — individually or as a ready-made kit.

Do I need special gear to hike Mount Timpanogos?+

For the full Timpanogos summit you'll want a real daypack, trekking poles, plenty of water capacity and sun protection — and early or late season, traction gear for snowfields. We rent all of it.

How much does hiking gear cost to rent?+

Most hiking items rent for $8–$40 per weekend. A small refundable deposit is placed on your card and released when you return the gear in good condition.
