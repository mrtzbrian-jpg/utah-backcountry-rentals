Where to camp near Utah County

Popular campgrounds & alpine spots

American Fork Canyon

Tibble Fork & Granite Flat

Alpine reservoir camping under Mount Timpanogos, about 30 minutes from Lehi. Great first stop for a quick overnighter or paddle day.

Uinta Mountains

Mirror Lake Highway

High-alpine lakes along Scenic Byway 150 — Trial Lake, Mirror Lake and Moosehorn. Cool summer nights and world-class stargazing.

Nebo Loop

Payson Lakes

Forested lakeside sites off the Nebo Loop Scenic Byway south of Payson — shaded, family-friendly and easy to reach.

Provo Canyon

Deer Creek Reservoir

Reservoir camping and paddling between Provo and Heber, with Mount Timpanogos as your backdrop.

Provo

Utah Lake State Park

Easy lakeside camping right in town — perfect for a low-key first night out or a paddleboard weekend.

Spanish Fork

Diamond Fork & Hobble Creek

Canyon campgrounds near the Fifth Water Hot Springs trail — a local favorite for a soak-and-sleep weekend.

Camping rental FAQs

Where can I rent camping gear in Utah County?+

Take a Hike Rentals rents camping gear with local pickup in Saratoga Springs, Utah — central to Lehi, American Fork, Provo, Orem and all of Utah County. Reserve online and grab your gear on your trip dates.

What camping equipment can I rent?+

Tents, sleeping bags, camp stoves, coolers, folding tables, bear canisters and portable power stations — individually or as a complete weekend bundle.

How much does it cost to rent camping gear?+

Most items rent for $8–$40 per weekend and full camping bundles start around $49. A small refundable deposit is placed on your card and released when you return the gear in good condition.
