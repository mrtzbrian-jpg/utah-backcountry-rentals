Visitor FAQs

Can I rent camping gear for a Utah vacation instead of flying with it?+

Yes — that's exactly what we do. Reserve tents, sleeping bags, backpacks, stoves and coolers online before your trip, pick everything up in Saratoga Springs (about 40 minutes from Salt Lake City International Airport), and return it before you fly home.

How far is pickup from Salt Lake City airport?+

Saratoga Springs is roughly a 40-minute drive from SLC airport — an easy stop on your way south toward Utah's national parks or east into the Wasatch and Uinta mountains.

Is rented gear good enough for Utah's national parks?+

Everything we rent is trail-tested, inspected and cleaned between trips — the same tents, bags and packs we'd take to Zion, Bryce Canyon, Arches, Canyonlands or Capitol Reef ourselves.

Can a family visiting Utah rent everything in one booking?+

Yes — our Family Weekend bundle covers 2 adults and 2 kids with tents, sleeping bags, packs, water filtration and safety gear in a single reservation. Solo and couples bundles are available too.
