Minutes from your driveway to the trailhead

Take a Hike Rentals is based in Saratoga Springs, right in the heart of northern Utah County. That puts quality rental gear about 10–20 minutes from Lehi, Eagle Mountain and American Fork, and an easy drive from Pleasant Grove, Lindon, Orem and Provo. Grab your kit on the way to American Fork Canyon, Provo Canyon, the Nebo Loop or the Uintas.

We rent tents (2–10 person), backpacks, sleeping bags, camp stoves, coolers, power stations and satellite communicators — individually or as complete bundles for solo hikers, couples and families. Everything is inspected, cleaned and packed before pickup.

Utah County rental FAQs

Is there a gear rental shop near Lehi or American Fork?+

Yes — Take a Hike Rentals is based in Saratoga Springs, about 10–20 minutes from Lehi, American Fork and Eagle Mountain. Reserve camping and hiking gear online and pick it up by appointment.

What can I rent in Utah County?+

Tents from 2 to 10 person, backpacks, sleeping bags, camp stoves, coolers, power stations, satellite communicators — plus complete ready-made bundles for solo hikers, couples and families.

Do you serve Provo and Orem?+

Absolutely. Saratoga Springs pickup is an easy drive from Provo and Orem, and most customers grab gear on their way to American Fork Canyon, the Nebo Loop or the Uintas.

How much does it cost?+

Individual items run $8–$40 per weekend and complete bundles start around $49, with a small refundable deposit released when you return the gear in good condition.
