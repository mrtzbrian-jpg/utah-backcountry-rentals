Tent rental FAQs

Where can I rent a tent in Utah County?+

Take a Hike Rentals rents tents with local pickup in Saratoga Springs, Utah — convenient for Lehi, American Fork, Provo, Orem and all of Utah County. Reserve online and pick up on your trip dates.

What size tents can I rent?+

Everything from lightweight 2-person backpacking tents to roomy 10-person instant cabin tents for family car camping.

How much does it cost to rent a tent?+

Tent rentals typically run $8–$40 per weekend depending on size, with a small refundable deposit that's released when you return it in good condition.

Are the tents clean and set up easily?+

Yes — every tent is inspected and cleaned between trips, and we'll walk you through setup so you're ready at the campsite.
